About Us

WELCOME TO PONDIT

Pondit Training School was founded by experienced information technology professionals and instructors who decided to concentrate on the hottest and fastest growing Information Technology (IT) areas: E-Commerce, Enterprise, Resources Planning(ERP), Automated Software Testing, database administration and networking. Pondit Training School has been educating computer professionals for long time and has launched numereous successfull careers in the field of information technology. The company employees talented instructors who are seasoned IT professionals and bring depth of real workplaces knowledge to their classrooms. We have built modern training facilities and computer networks with "industrial strength" verssion of ERP and E-Commerce software and operating systems. Pondit Training School isconveniently located at Azompur, Uttara in Dhaka, Bangladesh. In addition nto taking classes, our students are encouraged to use the facilities labs as much as they can, even after graduation, in order to maintain and advanced their skills.
